Privacy Policy

Mendixo — Defect Management App

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mendixo ("the App"), developed by welyang (Antigravity), collects, uses, and handles your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Mendixo to submit or process a defect report, the App may collect:

  • Photos — images you upload from your camera or photo gallery to document a defect
  • Defect description text — written descriptions of the defect you submit
  • Person In Charge name — the name of the responsible individual entered by the user
  • Location metadata within the project — tower, floor, room, and work category information selected within the App

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used solely for the following operational purposes:

  • Recording and tracking construction defects within the project
  • Storing defect reports and photos for review by authorized project personnel
  • Enabling follow-up and resolution of reported defects

3. Data Storage

All data submitted through the App is stored in the following Google services operated by the project owner:

  • Google Sheets — defect report records (text data)
  • Google Drive — uploaded photos

Data is stored on Google's infrastructure and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Access to stored data is restricted to authorized project personnel only.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

Mendixo does not collect:

  • Account credentials or login information (the App has no login system)
  • GPS or precise location data
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Device advertising IDs
  • Browsing history or tracking data

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to operate the App (i.e., Google Sheets and Google Drive as described above) or as required by law.

6. Data Retention

Defect report data is retained for the duration of the construction project. Once the project is complete, data may be archived or deleted at the discretion of the project owner. You may request deletion of your submitted data by contacting us at the address below.

7. Children's Privacy

Mendixo is intended for use by construction project personnel only. The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect the data submitted through the App. Access to stored records is limited to authorized personnel. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
